## What I'm exploring

AI-generated UGC (user-generated content) style video ads — synthetic avatars, talking-head creatives, and AI video tools that can produce scroll-stopping ad content at scale without a film crew.

The angle: most of my products (MarketMyApp, Helpmarq, Noctiq, PadelUp) need paid distribution eventually. AI UGC is the fastest path to test creatives cheaply before committing budget to production.

## Why it's interesting

Traditional ad creative is expensive and slow. AI video tools have crossed a quality threshold where synthetic creatives are indistinguishable from real UGC in the feed — and can be A/B tested at a fraction of the cost.

## Current status

Exploring. Researching tools, studying what's working in the space, figuring out where it fits in the broader marketing stack for my products.
