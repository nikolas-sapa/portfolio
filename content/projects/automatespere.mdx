## What it is

AutomateSphere is an AI automation agency targeting the Greek market. Co-founded with George Apostolakis. We go into businesses, map their repetitive workflows, and replace them with n8n + Claude pipelines.

Not generic chatbots. Actual automation that touches real operations — lead capture, qualification, content, comms, delivery tracking.

## How we operate

Seven-stage pipeline from first contact to ongoing retention. Everything is documented, templated, and repeatable.

Leads come in, get qualified via AI-assisted discovery, then we propose a custom automation stack. Contracts handled through DocuSign (Greek legal format), delivery tracked on Monday.com, payments via Stripe.

## Current status

Operational. Slack workspace running, Notion pipeline fully documented, n8n integrations in progress.

## Stack

n8n (self-hosted), Claude API, HubSpot, DocuSign, Monday.com, Stripe, Calendly, Fathom.
