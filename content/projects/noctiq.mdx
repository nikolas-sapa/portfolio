## What it is

Noctiq is an iOS app for people who want to actually optimize their sleep — not just track it. AI coach, a structured 56-lesson education program ("Becoming a Sleep Athlete"), wearable integrations, and community.

The core insight: most sleep apps give you data and stop there. Noctiq tells you what to do with it.

## What it does

- AI sleep coach you can talk to, backed by your data
- 8-week structured program: Foundations → Circadian Rhythm → Environment → Recovery → Advanced Protocols
- Wearable integrations (multiple devices)
- Gadget control for smart home sleep optimization
- Community with posting and accountability

## Pricing

Free tier with basic tracking and 5 AI chats per day. Pro at $9.99/month or $59.99/year — unlimited AI, full programs library, all wearable connections, all widgets. 7-day free trial.

Phase 0. SwiftUI scaffold in place. Deep competitive and pricing research done. Spec locked.
