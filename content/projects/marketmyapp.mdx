## What it is

MarketMyApp is an AI co-pilot for indie founders who are building but not marketing.

Every week, it tells you exactly three things to do to move the needle on growth. Not a 47-step framework. Not a course. Just: this week, do these three things, in this order, for this reason.

## Who it's for

Solo founders and indie hackers who know how to build but keep pushing marketing to next week. Builders who are drowning in generic advice and just want someone to tell them what to actually do.

## How it works

You connect your app. The AI analyzes your product, your market, and what's working or not. It produces a short, opinionated brief: your three highest-leverage marketing actions for the week ahead. Nothing else.

> Stop overthinking. Start executing. Your AI co-pilot tells you exactly what to do this week.

## Current status

MVP is complete and live at [marketmyapp.vercel.app](https://marketmyapp.vercel.app). Currently in the validate phase — testing the core loop end-to-end with real users. Built alongside a stealth iOS app and Creator Roast starting April 2026.

## Stack

Next.js, Supabase, TypeScript, Groq API (with Claude fallback), Stripe, Resend.
