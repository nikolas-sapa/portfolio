## What it is

Padel Up is the all-in-one padel companion. Everything a player needs in one app — whether you're a beginner trying to understand the basics or a competitive player training seriously.

## Features

- **Custom training plans** — built around your level, goals, and schedule
- **Video analysis** — upload match footage, get positioning and technique feedback
- **Court finder** — find courts near you
- **Nutrition** — fuel plans for training and match days
- **Learning hub** — tactics, technique guides, rule explanations
- **Progress tracking** — see improvement over time

The idea: stop switching between five different apps and coaches. Have one place that actually knows your game.

## Current status

Product is complete. Landing page live at [trypadelup.com](https://www.trypadelup.com/). Pre-launch — working through the App Store submission process.

## Stack

Swift, SwiftUI, Supabase, PostHog, iOS 17+.
