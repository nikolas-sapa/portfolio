> Plugins bundle agents, skills, commands, and hooks around a theme — PR review, spec-driven dev, TDD, testing. Install one and get a whole workflow out of the box. A handful earn their weight; most need trial-and-error to find what fits.

---

All plugins are installed at **user scope** (`~/.claude/plugins/cache/`).
